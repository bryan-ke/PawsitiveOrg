What We Do

At Pawsitive Org, we believe every stray deserves a second chance. Based in the Philippines, our mission is to rescue abandoned and neglected dogs and cats, giving them a safe shelter, proper care, and the love they deserve. More than just rescuing, we work hard to help each animal find a new beginning—with families ready to give them forever homes.



RESCUE

We actively search for stray and injured animals across urban streets, alleys, and rural areas in the Philippines. Whether they've been abandoned or born in the wild, our team ensures they're safely transported to our shelter, away from traffic, abuse, and hunger.

REHABILITATE

Once rescued, our animals receive full medical checkups, vaccinations, and treatment for injuries or illnesses. They're given proper food, a clean and safe space to rest, and daily interaction with caretakers—helping them rebuild trust in people and regain their health.

REHOME

Our adoption program carefully matches animals with loving families ready to commit to lifelong care. We provide support throughout the process, including home checks and education on pet care, to make sure every rehomed animal thrives in a stable, happy environment.

FAQs
Q: How can I adopt a dog or cat from Pawsitive Org?
A: Just head to our adoption page and fill out the form. Once we review your application, we'll contact you to schedule a visit and help match you with a furry friend that suits your lifestyle.
Q: Do you accept stray animals brought in by individuals?
A: Yes, we do! If you've found a stray in need, please contact us first so we can assess our current shelter capacity and advise you on the next steps.
Q: How can I volunteer with Pawsitive Org?
A: We'd love your help! Volunteers assist with feeding, cleaning, walking, and socializing the animals. Just sign up through our volunteer page and we'll get in touch with more details.
Q: Can I donate items instead of money?
A: Absolutely! We always need pet food, blankets, cleaning supplies, and medicine. Check out our donation list for what we're currently accepting.
Q: Where is your shelter located?
A: We're based in Angeles City, Pampanga, but we occasionally do outreach and adoption drives in nearby areas and within NCR. Follow us on social media for updates!
